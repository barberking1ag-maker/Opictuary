# PROVISIONAL PATENT APPLICATION

## Patent Title: Pre-Authorized Posthumous Content Vault and Release Management System

---

## ABSTRACT

A computer-implemented system and method for managing pre-authorized content created by public figures intended for release after their death. The system provides secure storage, access controls, scheduled release mechanisms, estate authentication, and fan engagement features allowing celebrities to curate their posthumous digital legacy.

---

## BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION

This invention relates to digital estate management and celebrity content systems. Prior art lacks systems for celebrities to pre-authorize content release after death with proper authentication and fan engagement features.

---

## SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION

System enabling celebrities to:
1. Create and store posthumous content
2. Schedule release dates and conditions
3. Control who can approve releases
4. Manage exclusive fan content
5. Generate charitable impact through memorial tributes

---

## DETAILED DESCRIPTION

### Components
- Content upload and storage
- Estate authentication system
- Release scheduling engine
- Fan engagement platform
- Charitable integration
- Admin controls
- Analytics dashboard

### Content Types
- Video messages
- Photo galleries
- Tributes and retrospectives
- Exclusive interviews
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Charity announcements
- Legacy statements

### Release Mechanisms
- Automatic release on specified date
- Conditional release (upon verification of death)
- Estate-authorized manual release
- Milestone-based releases
- Renewable content with expiration

## CLAIMS

**Claim 1:** A system for managing posthumous celebrity content comprising:
- secure content upload and storage;
- multi-factor estate authentication;
- scheduled or conditional content release;
- fan engagement and interaction platform;
- charitable donation integration;
- access control and privacy management;
- comprehensive audit logging.

**Claim 2:** The system of Claim 1, wherein content is released:
- on predetermined dates;
- upon verified death notification;
- with estate administrator approval;
- with automatic social media distribution;
- with accompanying charitable appeals.
