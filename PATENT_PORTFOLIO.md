### Patent #1: QR-Activated Video Time Capsule System
**Application #**: US-2024-0001
**Filed**: January 2024
**Status**: Provisional Filed

#### Abstract
A system and method for activating multimedia content through QR code scanning at physical memorial locations, with privacy-compliant analytics and contextual content delivery based on scanner identity and location.

#### Key Claims
1. QR code generation linked to specific memorial identifiers
2. Contextual content delivery based on:
   - Scanner relationship (family, friend, visitor)
   - Geographic location of scan
   - Time-based content release
3. Privacy-compliant geolocation tracking with explicit consent
4. Analytics dashboard for memorial administrators
5. Multi-purpose QR codes (tombstone, memorial card, event)

#### Commercial Applications
- Cemetery partnerships for smart tombstones
- Memorial card distribution at funeral services
- Location-based memorial experiences
- Analytics for cemetery management

#### Competitive Advantage
First system to combine QR activation with relationship-based content delivery and privacy-compliant tracking
