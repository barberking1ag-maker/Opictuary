## ðŸš€ STEP-BY-STEP USPTO WEBSITE INSTRUCTIONS

### STEP 1: Go to USPTO Website
1. Open browser and go to: **www.uspto.gov**
2. Click on **"Patents"** at the top menu
3. Click on **"File Online"**
4. Select **"EFS-Web"** (Electronic Filing System)

### STEP 2: Create Account (if you don't have one)
1. Click **"New User? Register Here"**
2. Fill in your name, email, password
3. Verify your email
4. Log back in

### STEP 3: Start New Application
1. After login, click **"File a New Application"**
2. Select **"Provisional Application for Patent"**
3. Click **"Continue"**

### STEP 4: Application Data
1. **Title of Invention:** Copy and paste: `System and Method for Secure Memorial Access by Incarcerated Individuals`
2. **Attorney Docket Number:** Type: `OPICT-001`
3. Click **"Continue"**

### STEP 5: Applicant Information
1. Click **"Add Applicant"**
2. Select **"Person"** (not company)
3. Enter your full legal name
4. Enter your address, phone, email
5. Click **"Add"**
6. Click **"Continue"**

### STEP 6: Micro Entity Status (Important - Saves Money!)
1. Select **"Yes, I qualify for micro entity status"**
2. Check the box that says you meet the requirements:
   - Made fewer than 4 previous patent applications
   - Gross income less than 3x the median household income (~$200,000)
   - Have not assigned rights to a large entity
3. Click **"Continue"**

### STEP 7: Upload Patent Document
1. Click **"Add Document"**
2. Select Document Type: **"Specification"**
3. Click **"Browse"** and upload the patent document (I'll prepare this for you)
4. Click **"Continue"**

### STEP 8: Review Fee
1. Confirm fee shows: **$65** (micro entity rate)
2. If it shows more, go back and fix micro entity status
3. Click **"Continue"**

### STEP 9: Submit and Pay
1. Review everything one last time
2. Click **"Submit Application"**
3. Enter payment information (credit card)
4. Click **"Pay $65"**
5. **SAVE YOUR CONFIRMATION NUMBER!**
