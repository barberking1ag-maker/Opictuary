# QR Book-Shaped Memorial Case Product Analysis

## Executive Summary

This analysis evaluates the feasibility of manufacturing and selling book-shaped memorial cases with embedded QR codes linking to digital memorial pages. The product represents a unique intersection of physical memorial keepsakes and digital memorial technology.

---

## Product Concept

### Description
A book-shaped decorative case (approximately 6" x 8" x 2") designed to sit on shelves or mantles, featuring:
- Durable outer shell mimicking a classic book spine
- Embedded QR code on the front or inside cover
- Optional photo frame insert
- Compartment for storing mementos, ashes, or keepsakes
- Adhesive backing option for permanent mounting

### Target Markets
1. **Direct Consumers:** Families seeking meaningful memorial keepsakes
2. **Funeral Homes:** Add-on product for service packages
3. **Cemetery/Cremation Providers:** Urn alternatives or accompaniments
4. **Memorial Gift Buyers:** Friends and extended family purchasing sympathy gifts

**Identified Partner:** Our Tributes (mentioned in research)
- Marine-grade steel QR plaques
- Wholesale pricing available
- Established manufacturing processes
- Quality already proven

**Advantages:**
- No manufacturing capital required

**Disadvantages:**

**Estimated Economics (Partner Model):**
```

Suggested retail price:     $79-129
Gross margin:               $34-84 (43%-65%)
```

### Option B: Custom Manufacturing (Overseas)

**Process:**
1. Product design and prototyping
2. Alibaba/Made-in-China manufacturer sourcing
3. Sample production and quality testing
4. Minimum order quantity (MOQ) production
5. Import and inventory management

**Key Considerations:**
- MOQ typically 500-1,000 units
- 45-90 day production timeline
- Quality control challenges
- Customs and shipping logistics

**Estimated Economics (Custom Manufacturing):**
```
Unit manufacturing cost:
├── Metal/alloy case:           $8-15
├── QR code engraving:          $1-3
├── Photo frame insert:         $2-4
├── Packaging:                  $2-5
├── Shipping (per unit):        $3-8
└── Total landed cost:          $16-35

Suggested retail price:         $79-129
Gross margin:                   $44-113 (56%-88%)
After platform/marketing:       $30-80 net per unit
```

### Option C: Domestic Manufacturing

**Advantages:**
- Higher quality control
- Faster iteration
- Smaller batch sizes possible
- "Made in USA" premium

**Disadvantages:**
- Higher costs (2-3x overseas)

- Longer setup time

**Estimated Economics (Domestic):**

## Risk Analysis

### High Risk
- **Inventory Obsolescence:** Product may not sell
- **Quality Issues:** Overseas manufacturing defects
- **Competition:** Larger players may copy concept

### Medium Risk
- **Margin Compression:** Price competition
- **Supply Chain:** Manufacturing delays
- **Technology Changes:** QR obsolescence (unlikely near-term)

### Low Risk
- **Market Demand:** Memorial market is stable
- **Platform Integration:** Technical integration straightforward
- **Regulatory:** No significant compliance barriers

### Mitigation Strategies
1. Start with partner model to validate before inventory investment
2. Small batch testing before large orders
3. Multiple supplier relationships
4. Patent protection for unique designs
