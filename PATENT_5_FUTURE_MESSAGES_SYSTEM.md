# PROVISIONAL PATENT APPLICATION

## Patent Title: Scheduled Post-Mortem Message Delivery System with Conditional Release

---

## ABSTRACT

A computer-implemented system for scheduling and delivering messages on behalf of deceased individuals to designated recipients. The system enables family members to compose messages scheduled for delivery on specific dates, anniversaries, milestones, or upon verification of the sender's death, with rich media support and personalized delivery options.

---

## BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION

This invention relates to memorial systems and automated messaging. Prior art lacks systems specifically designed to deliver messages from deceased persons on meaningful dates or milestones.

---

## SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION

System enabling:
1. Message scheduling for future delivery
2. Conditional delivery upon death verification
3. Milestone-based delivery (birthdays, anniversaries)
4. Rich media attachment support
5. Recipient management
6. Message templates
7. Delivery confirmation tracking

## CLAIMS

**Claim 1:** A method for scheduling post-mortem message delivery comprising:
- receiving message content from authorized family member;
- specifying recipient(s);
- defining release condition (date, anniversary, milestone);
- attaching media (photos, videos, audio);
- securely storing message;
- automatically delivering message when condition is met;
- confirming delivery to sender.

**Claim 2:** The method of Claim 1, wherein release conditions include:
- specific calendar date and time;
- anniversary of sender's death;
- recipient's birthday;
- years since sender's death;
- manual trigger by executor.

**Claim 3:** System for managing posthumous messages comprising:
- message composition and editing interface;
- scheduling and condition configuration;
- secure encrypted storage;
- automated delivery system;
- multi-channel delivery (email, SMS, app);
- confirmation and tracking.
