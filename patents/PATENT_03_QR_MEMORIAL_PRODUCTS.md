## TITLE OF INVENTION

**System and Method for E-Commerce Platform Enabling Customized Physical Memorial Products with Embedded QR Codes Linking to Digital Memorial Pages**

---

## FIELD OF THE INVENTION

The present invention relates to the integration of physical memorial products with digital memorial platforms, specifically to a comprehensive e-commerce system enabling the design, customization, ordering, and fulfillment of physical memorial products embedded with QR codes that link to persistent digital memorial pages.

---

## BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION

### The Problem

The death care industry faces a fundamental disconnect between physical memorials and digital preservation:

1. **Physical Memorials Are Static:** Traditional headstones, plaques, and monuments display only limited text and cannot be updated or expanded.

2. **Digital Memorials Lack Physical Presence:** Online memorial pages exist only in the digital realm, without tangible objects families can visit, touch, or place at meaningful locations.

3. **No Unified Commerce Platform:** Families must separately purchase physical memorials and create digital memorials, with no integration between them.

4. **Fragmented Supply Chain:** Memorial product vendors (headstone manufacturers, plaque makers, keepsake creators) operate independently from digital memorial platforms.

5. **QR Adoption Gap:** While QR codes are increasingly common, no comprehensive system exists for memorial-specific QR product commerce with integrated digital platform hosting.

### Prior Art Deficiencies

**Traditional Monument Companies:** Sell physical products without digital integration or QR functionality.

**Generic QR Product Vendors:** Offer QR-labeled products but without memorial-specific design, hosting, or perpetual maintenance.

**Digital Memorial Platforms (Legacy.com, etc.):** Focus on digital content without physical product offerings.

**Etsy/Amazon Memorial Products:** Generic marketplace without platform integration, custom QR generation, or memorial hosting.

**No existing system combines:** (a) memorial-specific product catalog, (b) QR code generation linked to digital memorials, (c) product customization wizard, (d) order management with manufacturer fulfillment, (e) perpetual QR hosting guarantee, and (f) integrated payment processing.

---

## SUMMARY OF THE INVENTION

The present invention provides:

1. A comprehensive **product catalog** of QR-embedded memorial products (plaques, headstones, jewelry, keepsakes)
2. A **customization wizard** for personalizing products with names, dates, photos, and QR placement
3. **Automated QR generation** linking to existing or new digital memorial pages
4. **Order management** with manufacturer partnership fulfillment
5. **Payment processing** with platform revenue model
6. **Perpetual QR hosting** ensuring links remain active indefinitely

## CLAIMS

### Independent Claims

**Claim 1:** A computer-implemented e-commerce system for QR-embedded memorial products, comprising:
   a) A product catalog module displaying memorial products with QR embedding options;
   b) A customization wizard enabling personalization of products with text, imagery, and QR placement;
   c) A QR generation engine creating unique codes linked to digital memorial pages;
   d) An order management system tracking products from purchase through delivery;
   e) A manufacturer integration layer enabling fulfillment through partner networks.

**Claim 2:** A method for creating and fulfilling customized QR memorial products, comprising:
   a) Receiving product selection from a user;
   b) Linking product to an existing or new digital memorial page;
   c) Collecting customization parameters including text, imagery, and QR placement;
   d) Generating a unique QR code with perpetual hosting commitment;
   e) Transmitting order to manufacturing partner;
   f) Tracking fulfillment through delivery.

**Claim 3:** A system for perpetual QR code hosting in physical memorial products, comprising:
   a) QR generation with guaranteed long-term URL persistence;
   b) Automated redirect capability if platform URLs change;
   c) Scan analytics tracking for memorial engagement;
   d) Endowment-backed hosting infrastructure for multi-generational persistence.

### Dependent Claims

**Claim 4:** The system of Claim 1, further comprising a proof approval workflow requiring customer confirmation before manufacturing.

**Claim 5:** The system of Claim 1, wherein the QR generation engine supports custom styling to match product aesthetics.

**Claim 6:** The method of Claim 2, wherein the manufacturing partner network includes plaques, jewelry, monuments, and specialty items.

**Claim 7:** The method of Claim 2, further comprising a 100-year hosting guarantee for QR code functionality.

**Claim 8:** The system of Claim 3, wherein scan analytics include geographic location, device type, and temporal patterns.

**Claim 9:** The system of Claim 1, further comprising integration with Stripe for payment processing and automatic commission splits.

**Claim 10:** A non-transitory computer-readable medium storing instructions for performing the method of Claim 2.
